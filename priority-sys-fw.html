Priority System

What is it?

  • The Priority System is a system to start with an abstract goal and end with concrete steps you can manage.
  • Transform your goal into an auto-generated visual road-map with clear objectives you can track.
  • See a clear timeline of your tasks so you can take your goal one day at a time.

How can this help me?

  • The Priority System

What/How overview?

  • The Priority System breaks down your goals using the CLEAR framework.
  • C - Categorize your goals & break them into subtasks.
  • L - List what's holding you back.
  • E - Erase limits.
  • A - "Actionize" goals using our auto-generated road-map.
  • R - Realize a timeframe by assigning dates and time-lines to a calendar.
